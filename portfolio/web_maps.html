Web Maps

Go By Bike USA

Functions as a repository for data relevant to cyclists, at a national scope. Statistics on cyclists mortality rate by state, best and worst cities for cycling and bicycle routes are complemented by a built-in routing machine and a base map that at the smallest scale, reveals points of interest for cyclists, such as public restrooms, coffee shops and pubs. Written in HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Urban Art Gallery

This is a prototype app created with Fulcrum and Carto.The idea is to have users take pictures of whatever they consider "art" throughout the city. Users submit pictures along with a questionnaire, with a mix of mandatory and open questions. The web map below displays the data collected from one test user. The map is hosted on my Carto page.
